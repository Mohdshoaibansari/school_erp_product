# School ERP SaaS Platform - Functional Requirements Catalog

## Purpose

This document serves as the master inventory of all functional capabilities that may exist within the School ERP SaaS platform.

The purpose is:

* Define product scope
* Identify module boundaries
* Guide roadmap planning
* Prevent requirement discovery from being lost

Inclusion in this catalog does not imply immediate implementation.

Some capabilities may be implemented years after platform launch.
